Why Babylons?

Babylons is a next generation NFT marketplace with low-cost minting, weekly rewards, and a DAO governance that puts the power in the hands of the community. Community meets NFTs. Only on Babylons.

A wide-spread community of creators and collectors already joined in. Contributers can enjoy lower costs at all stages of their experience in babylons while getting rewards by our genuine incentive mechanism. Lack of liquidity doesn’t exist in babylons due to our strong partnerships.

227,500

Weekly reward mechanism for active users

Each week, 227,500 tokens are distributed to active users according to their share in the weekly total volume. Both buyers and sellers are eligible. Weekly reward airdrops will be terminated after 365 weeks when all reserved tokens get distributed.

Conditions

First 3 months*

If weekly total volume exceeds 5,000 BNB, 227,500 tokens will be distributed.

Otherwise, a certain number of reserved tokens will be sent back to weekly reward pool proportional to missing volume.

First 3-6 months*

The limit will be updated to 10,000 BNB.

Limit

10,000 BNB

After first 6 months*

The limit will be updated to 15,000 BNB.

Limit

15,000 BNB

* Given values and ratios are subject to governance committee decisions.

Weekly profit sharing structure

Each week's income will be distributed to stakeholders (except locked tokens) as following.

Token Stakers

Token stakers get 30% as rewards.

Operations

25% will go towards ongoing business and marketing operations

Stored for buyback

25% will be used for buyback and buybacked tokens will be stored by founders.

Buybacked tokens will be stored at the address and will be locked by a smart contract for a certain time.

Burned

20% will be used for buyback and buybacked tokens will be burned.

Half of the burnable coins will be burned weekly. Remaining halves will be held and burned all together in big burn events every 6 months

* Given values and ratios are subject to governance committee decisions.

FAQ

What is Babylon’s?

Babylon’s is the first community owned marketplace built on Binance Smart Chain. Babylon’s NFT enables community to be a part of the marketplace.

Why BSC?

Because of the high fees in ETH chain, creators can not unleash their full potentials, also high gas fee restricts the market and give less to creators apart from they deserve.

What is $BABI?

Babi is the governance token of the Babylon’s. With $BABI community can decide the future of the marketplace. Also $BABI holders will get rewards from the profit.

How can I get $BABI?

Buyers and sellers will be rewarded with $BABI each week or you can buy from BSC DEX.

What is minting?

Minting refers to digitalizing your work using smart contracts.

What is “unlockable content”?

Creators can add content to their NFT, that is only visible to ownerships of the NFT.

How does the royalty system work?

One of the things that makes NFT ecosystem valuable is the royalty system. Creators can set a certain percentage as a royalty for secondary sales. For example: Creator set royalty to %10 and buyer of the NFT sells it to different buyer to 10 BNB. Creator will get 1 BNB commission from the secondary sale. With this feature creators will always get their share from the added value.
